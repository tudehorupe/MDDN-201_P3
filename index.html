Through walking or cycling, this presents the most viable option for long term sustained improvements. Not only is it better for our eco as well as our health, but it also presents a more cost-effective option. If a vehicle is required however, through carpooling, using public transport, and planning trips in advance, these options offer assistance for reducing impacts.

The consumption of particular foods such as red meats cause considerable damage to our eco. The packaging of various foods also present unsustainable long-term outcomes throughout their lifecycle. The use of reusable packaging/bags and buying in bulk are just some of many ways to better the environment.
